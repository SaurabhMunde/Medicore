Why Medicore...?


During past two decades internet has become an accepted way to purchase products and services. Buying medicines online are no exception. Besides its benefits, there are several risks of such purchases. So presenting MEDICORE which provides consumer a platform to select, compare and search medicines according to its benefits, side effects, uses, dosages and prices. Which help consumers to find the most appropriate medicine to satisfy its need knowing all facts about it.
